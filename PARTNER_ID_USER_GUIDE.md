# Partner ID System - User Guide

## What is a Partner ID?

A Partner ID is a unique 7-character code assigned to each therapist (partner) in the Therapy Tracker system. It consists of:
- **2 uppercase letters** (from the organization's name)
- **5 digits** (randomly generated)

**Example:** `AB12345`

---

## For Therapists (Partners)

### Finding Your Partner ID

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to your dashboard
3. Your Partner ID is displayed prominently at the top of the page
4. Click the **copy icon** (ðŸ“‹) next to your Partner ID to copy it to your clipboard

### Sharing Your Partner ID

Share your Partner ID with your patients/clients so they can sign up and automatically be linked to you:

- **Via Email:** Copy and paste your Partner ID in an email
- **Via Text/WhatsApp:** Send your Partner ID directly
- **In Person:** Write it down or show it on your screen
- **On Business Cards:** Consider printing it on your materials

**Important:** Keep your Partner ID accessible as new patients will need it to sign up.

---

## For Patients (Users)

### Signing Up with a Partner ID

1. Go to the signup page
2. Select **"User/Patient"** as your account type
3. Fill in your personal information
4. **Enter the Partner ID** provided by your therapist in the "Partner ID" field
   - Format: 2 uppercase letters + 5 digits (e.g., `AB12345`)
5. Complete the rest of the form
6. Click "Sign Up"

### What Happens After Signup?

- The system verifies your Partner ID
- If valid, your account is created
- You are automatically linked to your therapist
- You can start tracking your therapy progress immediately

### Common Errors

**"Partner ID is required"**
- You must enter a Partner ID to sign up as a patient
- Contact your therapist to get their Partner ID

**"Partner ID must be 2 uppercase letters followed by 5 digits"**
- Check the format of the Partner ID you entered
- It should look like: `AB12345` (2 letters, then 5 numbers)
- Make sure there are no spaces or special characters

**"Invalid Partner ID. Please check and try again."**
- The Partner ID you entered doesn't exist in the system
- Double-check the Partner ID with your therapist
- Make sure you typed it correctly (letters are uppercase)

## For Organizations

### Viewing Partner IDs

1. Log in to your organization account
2. Go to your dashboard
3. All your therapists are listed with their Partner IDs
4. Click on a therapist to see their Partner ID and assigned clients

### Partner ID Format

Partner IDs are automatically generated when a therapist signs up:
- The first 2 letters come from your organization's name
- Example: If your organization is "Wellness Center", Partner IDs will start with "WE"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change my Partner ID?**
A: Partner IDs are permanent and cannot be changed. They are unique identifiers in the system.

**Q: What if I lose my Partner ID?**
A: Simply log in to your dashboard - your Partner ID is always displayed there.

**Q: Can multiple patients use the same Partner ID?**
A: Yes! Your Partner ID is meant to be shared with all your patients. Each patient who signs up with your Partner ID will be automatically linked to you.

**Q: Is my Partner ID case-sensitive?**
A: The system automatically converts Partner IDs to uppercase, so `ab12345` and `AB12345` are treated the same.

**Q: What if I enter the wrong Partner ID?**
A: If you enter an invalid Partner ID, the system will not allow you to sign up. You'll need to get the correct Partner ID from your therapist.

**Q: Can I sign up without a Partner ID?**
A: No, patients must have a Partner ID to sign up. This ensures you're connected with a therapist from the start.

## Support

If you encounter any issues with Partner IDs:
1. Verify the Partner ID format is correct (2 letters + 5 digits)
2. Confirm with your therapist that you have the correct Partner ID
3. Contact your organization's administrator for assistance
4. Check that you're entering the Partner ID in the correct field during signup

---

## Security Note

Partner IDs are not sensitive information - they are meant to be shared. However:
- Only share your Partner ID with legitimate patients
- Organizations can track which patients are linked to which therapists
- All signup activity is logged for security purposes
