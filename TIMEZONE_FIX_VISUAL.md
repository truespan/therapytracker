```javascript
  `${formData.appointment_date} ${formData.appointment_time}:00`;
// Result: "2024-11-21 11:00:00" (Local time)
```

## Why IST Was Being Subtracted

**IST (India Standard Time) = UTC + 5:30**

When you use `.toISOString()`:
- It converts the local time to UTC
- UTC = Local Time - Timezone Offset
- UTC = 11:00 AM - 5:30 = 5:30 AM

That's why 11:00 AM became 5:30 AM!

## The Fix in One Sentence

**We stopped converting the time to UTC and now send it as-is to the backend.**
